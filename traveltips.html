Travel Tips

Currency

Currency used in Monaco is Euro. There are agents and Banks availble in Monaco to exchange any currency

Health

In case of emergency the visitors can dial 18 for Ambulences. The main hospital called "Princesse Grace" located in Avenue Pasteur.

Language

The main languge spoken in Monaco is French. The other spoken languages are Italian and English.

Eating

Monaco is sorrounded by many restaurants. The tourists can taste French,Italian,Japenese and Chinese dishes in Monaco. The French town called "Beausoleil" is at visinity. Its restaurents serve Portugese and Indian dishes.

Taxis

There is a well functioned taxi sevice in Monaco. The rates can be varied according to the travel time. info
