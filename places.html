Monaco from tourist camera

Plais du Prince

Prince's Palace is open to public between 10am to 5pm. Tickets: Adults:8€, Children:4€. Tickets must be booked online. info

Monaco Cathedral

Monaco Cathedral is built with white stone in 1875. It is open to public 9am to 9pm. Entrance Free. info

Monaco Opera

Opera House is also called Salle Garnier is open to public since 1879. The events calender is on the opera house website. info

Larvotto Beach

The beautiful clean water beach is in Larvotto. It attracts tousits because of Monaco's warm weather and the scenic sorroundings.

Ocenographic Museum

Established in 1910.It exhibits marine life including sharks,jellyfish, starfish turtles etc. Opening hours 9am to 6pm. Tickets: Adults 11€ Teens:7€. info

Jardin Exotic

The exotic garden is in Condamine. The garden has succulents from around the world. Opening hours 9am to 5pm. Tickets: Adults:7.20€, Children:3.80€. info

Monaco Habour

Monaco Habour is housed for luxury yachts and cruise ships. It organises the famous boat show every year. The Port is located in la Condamine. info

Monaco Casino

Monte-Carlo Casino is one of the main tourist attractions in monaco. The Entrants should follow a dress code. Opening hours:2pm to 2am. info
