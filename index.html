Monaco Monte-Carlo

Monaco Monte-Carlo is a city in French Riviera under the administration of French government. It has wonderful beaches and beautiful landscape. It attracts tourists around the world because of its year-round activities agenda.

Reigning monarch of the Monaco is Prince Albert II the son of Princesse Grace and Prince Rainier III. Monaco is famous for its annual Monaco Grand Prix and tax laws which facilitate millionaires residing in Monaco.
